Privacy Policy

Rabbit Breeding Calculator — last updated: 12 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Rabbit Breeding Calculator app ("the app") handles your information. Your privacy matters, and the app is designed to collect as little as possible.

Information the app stores

All data you enter — including your rabbits, breeding records, litters, health records, tasks, and financial entries — is stored only on your own device. This information is not transmitted to us or to any third party. We do not have access to it.

Information we collect

The app does not require an account and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or location. We do not use tracking or advertising technologies.

Backups

The app lets you export your data to a file that you control. When you export a backup, that file is saved wherever you choose. We never receive a copy of it.

Subscriptions

If you purchase a Pro subscription, the payment is processed by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment details. Google's handling of that transaction is governed by Google's own privacy policy.

Children

The app is a general-purpose tool and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
