Delete your account and data

App: Rabbit Breeding Calculator

How to request deletion

Email us at sarimportfolio@gmail.com from the email address you used to sign up, with the subject line "Delete my account".

We will delete your account within 30 days and email you to confirm.

What gets deleted

Everything associated with your account is permanently deleted:

  • Your name, email address, rabbitry name and profile photo
  • All rabbit records, photos, matings, litters, health records, tasks and financial entries
  • Your login credentials

What is kept

Nothing is kept after deletion, except where we are legally required to retain records. Backup copies are removed within 30 days.

Delete only some data

You can delete individual rabbits, litters, matings and other records inside the app at any time, without deleting your account.
